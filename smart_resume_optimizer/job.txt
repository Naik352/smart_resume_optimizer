Job description
Innovexis is excited to announce an opening for a Python Development Intern to join our team remotely.
This internship offers an excellent opportunity for aspiring software developers to gain hands-on experience by working on real-world applications in the Education Management industry.
You will contribute to building scalable solutions that support our digital platforms and learning systems.
As a Python Development Intern, you will work closely with experienced developers and product teams to design, develop, and optimize Python-based applications.
You will be involved in backend development, automation, and data-driven features that directly impact our educational products and services.
This internship is ideal for candidates who want to strengthen their Python skills while working in a flexible remote environment.
We offer a competitive stipend ranging from 20,000 to 25,000 per month, along with mentorship, live project exposure, and opportunities for professional growth during the internship.
Responsibilities.
Assist in developing and maintaining Python-based applications.
Write clean, efficient, and reusable Python code.
Work on backend logic, APIs, and integrations.
Debug, test, and optimize existing code.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements.
Participate in code reviews and team discussions.
Support automation and scripting tasks.
Document code, processes, and technical workflows.
Follow best practices for security, performance, and scalability.
Requirements.
Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
Strong foundation in Python programming.
Familiarity with frameworks such as Django or Flask (preferred).
Basic understanding of REST APIs, databases (MySQL/PostgreSQL), and Git.
Knowledge of data structures and problem-solving concepts.
Ability to communicate technical ideas clearly.
Self-motivated and able to work independently in a remote setup.
Strong time management and organizational skills.
